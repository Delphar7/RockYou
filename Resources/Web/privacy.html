Privacy Policy

RockYou by JTR

Last updated: January 7, 2026

RockYou is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it's used, and your rights.

The Short Version

RockYou collects no personal information. The app connects to Roku devices and TVs on your local network to send remote control commands. Settings can sync via your iCloud account (entirely within Apple's infrastructure). That's it.

What We Don't Collect

RockYou does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email, or any personal identifying information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device serial numbers or unique identifiers
  • Your viewing history or app usage patterns
  • Location data
  • Any data beyond what's needed for local device control

Local Network Access

RockYou requires local network access to:

  • Discover Roku streaming devices and compatible TVs on your Wi‑Fi network
  • Send commands (navigation, volume, power, etc.) to those devices
  • Receive status from devices (e.g., what app is running, whether text input is active)

All communication happens locally on your network. No data is sent to external servers or third parties.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud for RockYou in your device settings, the app stores:

  • Device pairings (which Roku goes with which TV)
  • App preferences (button hold settings, layout choices, etc.)
  • Most recently used apps per device

This data is stored exclusively in your private iCloud account using Apple's infrastructure. JTR does not have access to this data. You control iCloud sync via iOS/macOS settings and can disable it at any time.

Apple Services

RockYou uses standard Apple platform services:

  • App Store Connect: Apple collects anonymous usage statistics and crash reports to help improve the app. This is managed by Apple, not JTR.
  • TestFlight: If you're using a beta version, Apple provides crash and diagnostic data to help fix bugs before public release.

These services are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Future: Voluntary Device Support Data

In a future update, RockYou may offer an opt-in feature to help add support for additional TV brands. If implemented, this would allow you to:

  • Voluntarily gather technical information about your TV (model, supported commands, etc.)
  • Choose to send that data to JTR to improve device compatibility

This feature does not exist yet. If added, it would be entirely optional, clearly labeled, and only activated by your explicit action. No data would be collected without your knowledge and consent.

Third-Party Services

RockYou does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

RockYou does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is a simple remote control that works entirely on your local network.

Your Rights

Because RockYou collects no personal data:

  • There is no data to request, export, or delete from JTR
  • Your iCloud data is entirely under your control via Apple's settings
  • You can delete the app at any time to remove all local data

If you have privacy questions or concerns, you can contact us at privacy@RockYou.jtr.sh.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy (for example, if we add the voluntary device support feature described above), we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and may notify users through the app or App Store release notes.

Continued use of RockYou after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Email: privacy@RockYou.jtr.sh
